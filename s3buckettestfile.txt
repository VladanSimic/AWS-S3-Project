WATERFALL MODEL OF SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT:

The waterfall model takes place in a fixed order with rigid and formal rules, and the project development team only moves to the next stage of development or testing when the previous step has been successfully completed.



AGILE METHODS OF SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT:
Agile methodology supports the simultaneous iteration of development and testing in the software development process, which automatically leads to greater communication between clients, developers, managers and testers.
